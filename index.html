Our Story

The Importance Of The Housing Redevelopment Projects

Welcome to our real estate company, dedicated to providing affordable housing options to the residents of Soweto, South Africa. Our mission is to empower the local community by creating durable and high-quality RDP housing developments that are accessible to all.

As residents of Soweto ourselves, we understand the unique cultural and historical significance of this vibrant township. We are committed to uplifting the community and creating a brighter future for everyone who calls Soweto home.

Our team is comprised of experienced real estate professionals who are passionate about making a positive impact in Soweto. We believe that everyone deserves a safe and comfortable place to call home, and we are committed to making that a reality for as many families as possible.

Our Projects

Jabulani Housing Flats

The Jabulani redevelopment project forms part of governments Hostels Eradication programme and was approved for a total number of 663 units.Jabulani Ext. 1 Housing Project, consisting of 663 units.The Jabulani Housing Project will provide subsidised homes, benefitting people as well as catering for the gap market, the people who earn too much to qualify for a government housing subsidy but too little to qualify for a bank loan.

Fleurhof RDP Housing Units

The Fleurhof project is one of the City’s integrated residential mega projects which addresses a key strategic priority of the government of Local Unity.
​Fleurhof is one of Gauteng’s largest integrated housing projects that will bridge the gap between the rich and the poor. The project consists of various types of housing opportunities and forms of tenure. Approximately 112 units will be handed over to beneficiaries in phases over the next few weeks, with the process having commenced today.

The Lufhereng Housing Development

The Lufhereng Housing Development aims to address the housing shortage in the City.This housing project is part of the Lufhereng Integrated Housing Development, which is characterised by the provision and integration of various land uses and numerous housing opportunities for families with differing income levels offering an array of differing accommodation needs and tenure types. The project is planned and being implemented as a mixed use and fully integrated housing development delivering a full range of housing mix, tenure mix, land-use mix, income-earners mix, also linked to the provision of supportive social and economic amenities and infrastructure towards the fully sustainable housing model.
